EduC[alcul]ate

Description

Created as a tool to help students calculate their academic success, Edu[calcul]ate is a calulator app capable of multiple academic calculations.

Currently consisting of two primary functions, the app is able to calculate a student's mark based on weightings, as well as calculate their required exam mark based upon the end mark they would like to achieve.
